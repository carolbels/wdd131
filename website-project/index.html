Welcome to Data-Driven Rewards

My name's Jackson Aquino. I am often driven by curiosity when analyzing data and I am passionate about sharing knowledge with fellow professionals to help them enhance their hard skills. I have a deep interest in exploring economic theories and understanding how they shape people's perceptions of total rewards.

When I began my Data Science degree, I was working as an HR Generalist and considering a career shift outside of HR. However, after stepping into a compensation role, I quickly developed a profound interest in the field. I realized that instead of pursuing a purely data science role, I could leverage my data science skills to enhance and innovate within the realm of compensation.

Currently, I apply statistics and programming to transform how I address compensation challenges within my organization. My unique expertise positions me to effectively manage compensation programs across the Americas.
